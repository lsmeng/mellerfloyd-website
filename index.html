What We Do

Focused Representation for Property Owners

We represent landlords — and only landlords — so our entire practice is built around protecting your investment and your rights as an owner.

Evictions & Unlawful Detainer

Full-service handling of the eviction process — from notice to judgment to recovering possession — done correctly the first time to avoid costly delays.

Rent Control Compliance

Deep familiarity with Santa Monica and Los Angeles rent-stabilization rules — helping owners stay compliant and act within their legal rights.

Lease & Notice Drafting

Properly drafted leases, three-day notices, and termination notices that hold up in court and protect you before a dispute ever begins.

Rent & Debt Recovery

We don't stop at the judgment. We pursue unpaid rent and damages — and have a track record of actually collecting what you are owed.

Real Estate Disputes

Counsel and litigation on property ownership, contracts, title, and related real estate matters affecting your portfolio.

Landlord Advisory

Practical, plain-spoken guidance for owners and property managers on day-to-day questions before they escalate into litigation.

Why Owners Choose Us

Experience That Has Stood the Test of Time

Meller & Floyd has represented Southern California property owners since the 1980s. We know the courts, we know the local rent-control ordinances, and we know what it takes to move a case efficiently from notice to recovery.

Because we represent landlords exclusively, our interests are always fully aligned with yours — there are no divided loyalties and no conflicts.

  • Landlord-side only. We never represent tenants — your goals are our only focus.
  • We collect, not just win. A judgment is only worth what you can recover — and recovery is our priority.
  • Local rent-control fluency. Santa Monica and L.A. ordinances are our home turf.
  • Direct attorney access. You work with the partners, not a rotating cast.
